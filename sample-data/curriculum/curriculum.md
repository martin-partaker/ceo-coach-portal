# Curriculum snippets (synthetic)

These are *not* official ScaleOS materials. They’re safe placeholders that capture the patterns observed in the calls so the product can be implemented and demoed.

## The 10X Flight Plan (overview)

1. **Destination**: a precise 3-year outcome statement (the 10X goal).
2. **Business model redesign**: re-think **Product / Market / Delivery** to make the destination possible.
3. **80/20 Path + Math**: identify the handful of priorities that create most of the outcome; translate to simple math.
4. **People**: elevate leadership capacity and remove bottlenecks (including the CEO as bottleneck).
5. **Execution + Accountability**: weekly momentum journals + visible commitments.

## Constraint-led coaching

- Don’t try to optimize everything.
- Find the **single highest-leverage constraint**.
- Work on the constraint until it moves, then re-evaluate.

## Session outcome standard

If a client leaves:
- **focused and unenergized** → bust
- **energized and unfocused** → bust
- **focused and energized** → success

## Momentum journals (weekly)

The journal is a forcing function:
- closes the say/do gap
- makes progress visible
- reveals avoidance patterns early

## Coach stance

- Be a **devil’s advocate** (question assumptions).
- Don’t become a consultant.
- Require evidence-based commitments.
