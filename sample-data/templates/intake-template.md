# Client intake (template)

## Basics
- Name:
- Company:
- Industry:
- Timezone:
- Preferred session days/times:

## Current reality (today)
1. What is your current annual revenue (or budget, for nonprofit)?
2. What are your top 3 products/services/programs?
3. Who is your target customer/client/payer?
4. What is the current “delivery model” (how value gets delivered end-to-end)?

## Destination (10X goal)
1. What is your 3-year “10X destination” statement?
2. Why does it matter (mission / personal / strategic)?
3. What would have to be true for it to be achievable?

## Constraints (what is most limiting right now?)
1. What is the #1 constraint?
2. What have you tried already?
3. What are you avoiding?

## People & org
1. What key roles are missing?
2. Who do you currently rely on most?
3. Where are you the bottleneck?

## Commitment & integrity
1. If we look back in 90 days, what would “I did the work” look like?
2. What might stop you from doing the work?
3. How do you want your coach to hold you accountable?
