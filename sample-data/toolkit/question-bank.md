# Question bank (synthetic)

## Destination (10X goal)
- If you hit this destination, what’s *different* about your identity as a leader?
- What must be true about your business model for this to be real?
- What are you currently “loyal” to that the goal would force you to change?
- If this goal is non-negotiable, what becomes the obvious 80/20?

## Constraint-led coaching
- What is the single constraint that, if removed, makes everything else easier?
- What have you tried already? What didn’t work? Why?
- What conversation, decision, or hire are you avoiding?
- What are you optimizing that doesn’t matter at 10X scale?

## Product / Market / Delivery
- Product: Which product family is the “must win” wedge? What gets eliminated?
- Market: Who is the ICP at 10X? What customers become noise?
- Delivery: What channel/partner/system must exist to scale delivery?

## Commitments & accountability
- What are the 3 commitments you will complete in the next 30 days?
- How will we know (evidence, numbers, artifacts)?
- What will sabotage you? What’s the counter-plan?
- When exactly is your weekly momentum journal time-blocked?
